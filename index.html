About Us

Government Engineering College, Aurangabad, welcomed a spirited tradition in November 2024 - UMANG 24 (JUNOON), an annual sports fest that has quickly become a pivotal event in our academic calendar. Each year, the campus reverberates with energy and enthusiasm as students from all corners of our institution engage in a diverse array of sports, including Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Athletics, Kabaddi, Tug of War, Badminton, Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess, Arm Wrestling, Weightlifting, and Online Games. UMANG 24 (JUNOON)'s significance goes beyond the thrill of competition; it embodies a profound purpose. Our motto, "Mentally and Physically Fit," underscores our commitment to fostering holistic well-being. The fest serves as an arena where students not only chase physical fitness but also hone their mental tenacity. It's a poignant reminder that a healthy body and a resilient mind are inextricably linked. UMANG 24 (JUNOON)is a manifestation of the unwavering determination of our students and a testament to their dedication to living balanced, healthy lives. This annual extravaganza serves as a poignant reminder that, when ignited with passion and determination, there are no limits to what we can achieve. JUNOON is where the spirit of sports meets the pursuit of comprehensive well-being, a celebration of human potential and a tribute to the indomitable spirit of our institution.
